Tip Messages

While net diving, you may receive a message from yourself with a tip. There are 26 messages in total, tabulated below.

Topic
Description
Drivers and colors The colors of the Drivers indicate different approaches: Red for stat changes, yellow for Driver order, green for breeding, and blue for interactions with other Drivers. There are also other Drivers with unclear functions.
Driver sets Drivers are divided into sets, with web, store, and evolved Digimon Drivers being distinct. Rarer Drivers are generally better.
Fatigue Digimon fatigue limits actions. Time management is crucial before pausing or closing the game.
Mutants Mutant Digimon, like Numemon, have hidden potential and can achieve surprising forms.
Adaptability Players must adapt to available resources and "make do with what you have."
Strategies Driver synergies can be powerful, but their frequent breaking makes consistent execution difficult.
Drivers Drivers are essential for Digimon growth, but they are hard to obtain and break easily.
Freedom Evolution requirements are flexible, including stats, weight, and bond, but specific information is limited.
Maximization Reaching maximum training levels is challenging, and the methods used by top players are unclear.
Innate Power Digimon have innate power values based on their species, which vary between individuals.
Genetics Digimon inherit stats from their parents, and their Innate Power influences this inheritance.
Life cycle Digimon have a life cycle of birth, growth, evolution, death, and rebirth. Evolution extends their lifespan.
Hidden evolutions Side Evolutions allow Digimon to evolve at their current stage, increasing lifespan. Requirements and eligible Digimon are unknown.
Stealth battles Digimon remain connected to the server when the game is closed and can be attacked. Preparation is crucial before closing the game.
Crushing defeat Defeated Digimon with very low negative health suffer injuries or sickness.
Terminal virus Certain conditions can cause Digimon to become terminally ill until death or evolution.
Illness Medicine is essential for treating Digimon sickness, as untreated sickness is often fatal.
Injuries Bandages are necessary for treating injuries sustained in online battles.
Bond Digimon bond is affected by treatment and impacts stats, evolutions, and training.
Special condition Some Digimon have special evolution conditions, such as Tyranomon's balanced stats.
Farm The farm is a resource-gathering alternative, providing food over time.
Shop The shop's inventory and prices vary over time, and it closes for restocking.
Winrate Winrate is tied to evolution; failing to meet winrate requirements prevents evolution.
Battles Battles are essential for Digimon growth.
Training Balancing training and battles is important for Digimon development.
Feeding Different foods affect weight, training, bond, and mistakes, requiring experimentation to understand their effects.
